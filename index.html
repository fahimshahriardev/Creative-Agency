Brand Identity

In the digital era, an image is worth more than a thousand words, and we're here to help you stand out. From logo guides to brand analysis, to communication tone, we’ve got your branding covered..

UX & Graphic Design

Our user experience design team encompasses the complete user journey, incorporating aspects of branding, design, usability and function.

Digital Strategy

The brand strategy indicates the findings of brand research. While the research phase is about exploration, this step is for focusing and consolidating your values, aspirations, and vision for the future. The strategy stage sets the tone of your communication and provides a rallying point for your team in terms of brand values and campaign direction.

Web Development & Design

Positioning a business online can be a difficult and overwhelming task, but we can help you develop a digital strategy that fits your needs. Our service covers website accessibility, adaptability, usability, and SEO’s — go digital with us!

Advertising & Media

We help you optimize your marketing by creating and using multiple methods. Producing videos, developing advertising campaigns, and professional photography.

Social Media Management

To create content that matches your business’s brand, we have partnered with a social media software. This software provides the essential tools and analytics to adjust to your needs to speak visually to your target audience.

Automation

Understanding how your customers interact with your brand, providing them a proper customer experience, and personalized communication at every point of interaction through your customer journey is vital for your brand and for generating engagement with them. This demands our attention to customer needs, accuracy, and effective response times. Our new Marketing Automation Service goes into action allowing us to cover every point of the marketing funnel through automated and personalized communications, content, and determining the best time to reach your customer. Its purpose is to automate repetitive tasks to save you time and generate more revenue for your brand or company. Our service can include the development of email marketing, sales funnel, advertising funnel, billing, forms, segmentation & chatbots.

The D&S Agency Method

Discovery
Meeting

We will use this initial meeting to learn about your business and your marketing goals. We’ll discover your target audience and find out if your current strategy is working for you. Our goal is to gather vital information to achieve a deeper understanding of your business needs.

Analysis
& Proposal

In the analysis phase, we ensure that everyone comprehends the vision of the project. Once we have a clear scope of what your business needs, we provide you a project proposal that meets your requirements..

Implementation

Through this stage, our team takes action. We follow the necessary steps to turn your vision for the business into reality.

Monitoring
& Optimization

We ensure that our strategy is meeting expectations and reaching your target audience. Our on-going services include social media, graphic design, advertising campaigns, and website optimization. We monitor your website by identifying and resolving potential issues and ensure that it is always online and operating.
